Upcoming Events

Edgar County Shooting Sports Informational Meeting

Sunday, February 24th, 2 PM at the 4-H Multi-Purpose building. This meeting is for all youth, parents and potential volunteers. Enrollment forms for this club will be available at the meeting. Non-4-H members are welcome to join. There is a $20 fee for non-4-H members. Contact Chris Milam for more information at 822-9841.

Music Performance by Costas

Fling winter far away with some hot music! The Spurlock Museum celebrates the melodies of Colombia and Ecuador at an afternoon with the band Costas on February 24. At 1:00 PM, band members will discuss their musical instruments in the South American exhibit, and at 1:45, they will perform a concert in the Ancient Mediterranean Gallery.
