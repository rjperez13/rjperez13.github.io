Parent Gateway

Welcome to the Parent Gateway! As a parent of Illinois, we value your time, and appreciate your affiliation to the University of Illinois. We at Illinois strive to make not only your child’s time the best, but yours as well.

On the sidebar to your left, you can find tools to see news and upcoming events, pay your student's bills, learn more about financial aid, find out about student safety, join parent programs, or visit the university.

If you cannot find what you are looking for, please use the search bar at the top of the page, use the FAQ, or contact us for your more specific needs.
