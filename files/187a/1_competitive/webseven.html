Assignment 2 - Competitive Analysis Profile
Rodya J. Perez
rodyajperez@gmail.com

Brand: Experienced - They’ve been around since 2002, and have learned to adapt to the changing digital market.
They stick to tried and tested methods of design. They are trying to convey that they know what they are doing
Diversity - They have a team that is disciplined in multiple aspects
Trying to show that each member of their team brings something unique to the table, in order to create the best experience for their customers
Trendsetters - Although they stick to tried and tested methods, they are not afraid to break the mold in order to outsmart the competition
Functionality: Follows a three box layout, very simplistic
Most of the functionality happens through the fixed sidebar
They also use different layouts for different pages to exemplify the content
For example, using a gallery to display all their blog post
Interactivity: The navigation bar is horizontally displayed on the left as a side-bar pop out
Large buttons display important information
Pictures are scrollable from within the web page
Main page has a video playing
Navigation The fixed sidebar never moves and is discreet enough that it doesn’t become a nuisance
The footer is an alternative to navigation if one wishes not to use the sidebar
