Brand: Exquisite-Elegant-Bold Design - all keywords they emphasize on their homepage. They want to show that their designs stand out
Fancy Brewing Design - makes the parallel of their design style to crafting high quality beer
it denotes the time and effort they put into designing websites
Sophistication - The mascot is a man with a thick mustache, beer in hand, sharp haricut, and a monocle
they are selling this image to their customers
Functionality: Design is sleek
Borders and shades of blue work to separate content
Interactivity: When hovering over the navigation bar, a unique animation is paired with the mascot
Hovering over recent projects makes a sidebar pop out detailing the programs used to make that project
Navigation Header is used for navigation
Footer is used to access blog and twitter post
